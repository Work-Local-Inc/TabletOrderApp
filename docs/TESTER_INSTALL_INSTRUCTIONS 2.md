# Menu.ca Orders - Tablet App Installation

## Step 1: Open the Test Link
Click this link **on your tablet**:

https://play.google.com/apps/internaltest/4701568790980420051

## Step 2: Sign In
Sign in with the **Google account email we registered** (the email you provided to Menu.ca).

## Step 3: Confirm Tester Status
You should see a green badge that says **"You are a tester"**:

![You are a tester badge](../assets/tester-badge.png)

If you see this green badge, you're ready to install!

## Step 4: Download the App
Tap **"download it on Google Play"** and install the app.

---

## Troubleshooting

**Don't see the green badge?**
- Make sure you're signed into the correct Google account
- Contact Menu.ca support with your Google email address

**App shows old version?**
- Uninstall the app completely
- Reinstall from the link above
- Go to Settings in the app and toggle "Simplified View" ON

**Support:** Contact your Menu.ca representative if you have issues.
