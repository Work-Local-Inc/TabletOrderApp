# Privacy Policy for Menu.ca Orders

**Last Updated:** January 13, 2026

## Introduction

Menu.ca Orders ("the App") is a kitchen order management application designed for restaurant staff. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Device Information
- **Device Identifier:** A unique ID to authenticate your tablet with your restaurant's ordering system
- **Device Name:** For display purposes in the restaurant dashboard

### Order Data
- Order details (items, quantities, special instructions)
- Order status and timestamps
- This data is transmitted from your restaurant's Menu.ca ordering system

### Technical Data
- App crash logs and error reports (to improve app stability)
- Connection status and printer connectivity logs

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- Customer personal information (names, addresses, payment details)
- Location data
- Contacts or photos
- Browsing history

## How We Use Your Information

- **Authentication:** To verify your device is authorized for your restaurant
- **Order Display:** To show incoming orders on your kitchen tablet
- **Printing:** To send orders to your thermal printer
- **Support:** To diagnose issues if you contact support

## Data Storage & Security

- All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
- Order data is stored on secure servers managed by Menu.ca
- Device credentials are stored locally on your tablet in encrypted storage
- We do not sell or share your data with third parties

## Data Retention

- Order data: Retained according to your restaurant's settings
- Device authentication: Until you log out or deregister the device
- Crash logs: 90 days

## Your Rights

You can:
- Log out and remove all local data from the device
- Request deletion of your restaurant's data by contacting support
- Request a copy of data associated with your device

## Children's Privacy

This App is designed for business use by restaurant staff and is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the App or on our website.
